In addition to all of the above tools uxento offers, users can also take part in our referral program to take full advantage of all the benefits of the uxento ecosystem. Referrals are available across all uxento products (uDev + everything else we build).

## Cashback System:  
uxento's Cashback System is based upon your coin creation + buy/sell volume. Rewards are provided by uxento via our fees and does not take anything away from your Pumpfun/Bonk/Trading Platform's Rewards' Systems.

## Boosts:
uxento will offer event-like points boosters/multipliers to incentivize launching, and earning. To see if any boosts are currently active, visit your points homepage and navigate to System Info. Under the Boost Info panel, you will see information about any currently active boosts. 

## Leaderboard: 
The highest earners will find their place on uxento's Leaderboard, which displays the current top ten users of uxento, their rank and how many points they have accrued.
